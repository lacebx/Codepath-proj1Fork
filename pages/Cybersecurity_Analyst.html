Cybersecurity Analyst

Protect organizations from cyber threats by monitoring, analyzing, and responding to security incidents.

Job Overview

Cybersecurity Analysts are responsible for ensuring the safety of a company's information systems. They monitor for threats, perform vulnerability assessments, and implement security protocols to safeguard data.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Monitoring security systems for potential breaches
  • Investigating and responding to security incidents
  • Implementing security measures to protect sensitive information
  • Conducting regular security audits and risk assessments

Skills Required:

  • Proficiency in network security protocols
  • Experience with tools like firewalls, VPNs, and encryption
  • Understanding of ethical hacking and penetration testing
  • Certifications like CEH, CISSP, or CompTIA Security+

Salary Range:

Cybersecurity Analysts typically earn between $85,000 and $125,000 annually, depending on the company and experience level.
